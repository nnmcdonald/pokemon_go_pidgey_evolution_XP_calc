This progam calculates the maximum experience a player can earn in pokemon go for pidgey evolutions given
the number of pidgey and pidgey candies the player has. 12 candies were required to evolve a pidgey, 1 pidgey could be transferred 
for 1 candy, and 1 pidgeotto could be transferred for 4 candies. The program outputs the transfers and evolutions necessary to gain 
the most possible experience and outputs the total XP as well
